About ITCC

Overview

UniKL MIIT has successfully organized Information Technology Case Challenge (ITCC) In July 2017, received positive feedback from our sponsor and external judges from industry. This year, we are proudly expand ITCC'18 competition to be participated by university and school to the students.

The idea of this competition is to allow participants from any programs in UniKL MIIT to participate as a team in this event. This competition will assess students based on their cross functional knowledge in the area of Information Technology.

The aim of this competition is to help students to discover their innovative minds by allowing them to compete over case studies in the field of Information Technology. The case studies is specifically constructed by real companies and industries as a platform for the students to compete at International level.

The objectives of this competition are:

To apply student's knowledge and problem solving skills based in the case base situation through the competition. To provide opportunity for the student to gain experience by participating in IT related competitions.

This competition consist of three rounds for University Level. Over the two days' event participants will be given a case to work on and are required to prepare a solution based on the case provided. The participants will be assessed by professionals from the area of Information Technology from their showcase of problem solving and presentation skills prior to determine the best solutions as winner.
