Competition

Overview

Case competition is a competition where participants must develop a solution related to IT that can be beneficial to various industries within a designated time period. In this competition, teams will deliver presentation of their solution, which will be scored depending on the criteria of the competition. With the two-round competition scheme, it measures the depth of the participants' analysis about the case and how their solid strategy are being implemented to solve the problem, without concentrating only on the solutions provided. A panel of judges comprise of academics, IT and business professionals will do the scoring evaluation. Only teams with the best scores will be the winners.
